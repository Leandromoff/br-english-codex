Adjectives: old, interesting, expensive, etc.

Adjectives before a noun

An adjective usually comes before a noun.

This is a new phone.

She has a beautiful dress.

Adjectives after be

We can put an adjective after the verb be.

My car is old.

The weather is nice today.

Adjectives don't change

Adjectives are the same in singular and plural.

It's an interesting book.

They are interesting books.

Common adjectives

Some common adjectives are: old, new, big, small, young, tall, short, good, bad, beautiful, ugly, interesting, boring, expensive, cheap, clean and dirty.
