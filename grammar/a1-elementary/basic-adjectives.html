Adjectives: old, interesting, expensive, etc.

Adjectives describe people, places and things. In English they usually come before the noun or after the verb be.

1. Adjective + noun

The adjective goes before the noun it describes.

a big car

an interesting book

2. Be + adjective

We often use adjectives after be.

The car is big.

Her book is interesting.

3. Common adjectives and opposites

Adjective Opposite
oldnew
bigsmall
cheapexpensive
easydifficult
interestingboring

4. Nationality adjectives

Adjectives of nationality begin with a capital letter.

She is Brazilian.

They are Italian.
