Questions: word order and question words

Overview

English questions often follow the QWASI pattern. QWASI means Question Word + Auxiliary + Subject + Infinitive.

Where are you from?

What does she want?

Form

Type Word order Example
be Question word + am/is/are + subject Where are you?
do/does Question word + do/does + subject + verb What does he do?

Common question words

Question word Use
whatthings
whereplaces
whentime
whopeople
whyreasons
howmanner or method
whichchoice
whosepossession
